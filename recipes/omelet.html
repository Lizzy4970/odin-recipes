omelet

Description

The standard eggy breakfast item everone loves. Well maybe not Sashian but oh well. The most yummy and diverse bfreakfast item in existence makes a great sunday morning meal. even for dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • salt and pepper
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl
  2. Shake some salt and pepper into the eggs and mix
  3. add some cheese and mix
  4. add your omelet mixture in a hot greased pan and let all the bottom cook and then flip, and you are done!
